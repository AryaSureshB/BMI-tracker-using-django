BODY MASS INDEX(BMI) TRACKER

Tracking your BMI is a useful way of checking if you’re maintaining a healthy weight. It’s calculated using a person's weight and height,

using this formula: weight / height²

The resulting number indicates one of the following categories:

Underweight = less than 18.5

Normal = more or equal to 18.5 and less than 25

Overweight = more or equal to 25 and less than 30

Obesity = 30 or more

Here is the simple Bmi tracker to find your BMI quicker and easier, by taking a person's weight and height as input and outputs the corresponding BMI category.
